Modular

FLSE makes vanilla HTML modular, for better developed sites and shorter development times. Supress frequently used code into one, generate custom elements to match your needs, and with the power of Javascript enable rich handling of every element to the maximum.

Easy

FLSE is designed to accomodate your existing vanilla stack and designed to stay easy. Little to no additional HTML, CSS or JS knowledge is required to master the arts of FLSE. Simply learn to import, and you're good to go!
